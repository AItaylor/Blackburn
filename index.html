Our Work – Building Dreams, One Home at a Time

Scarborough Family Home (2020)

Brady rebuilt his own home in Scarborough from the ground up after purchasing a fixer-upper. He managed every aspect, from structural design to plumbing and electrical work, creating a modern, energy-efficient home tailored to his family’s needs. A client in Etobicoke hired Brady to renovate their outdated kitchen and add a new family room. Brady consulted on material choices, designed a layout to maximize natural light, and completed the project under budget, earning praise for his problem-solving and clean worksite.

In Jamaica, Brady led the construction of a 3-bedroom vacation home designed to withstand tropical weather. He incorporated local materials and sustainable practices, finishing the project in 12 months while navigating logistical challenges of working abroad.
